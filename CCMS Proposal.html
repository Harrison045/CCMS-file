02 · Strategic Context 04 / 11
The problem

Five gaps
the church
cannot close
without a system.

"Every week, members walk through a church door and never return — and no one knows."
01
No real-time visibility
District and national leaders wait on manual submissions; data arrives already stale.
02
No signal when members disengage
When attendance fades, no system detects it or triggers pastoral follow-up.
03
No reliable communication layer
WhatsApp groups and flyers reach only a fraction of the congregation.
04
No structured event coordination
Camp meetings and district programs are hard to publicize across locations.
05
No leadership intelligence
Pastors and admins operate without data on growth, engagement, or attendance.

03 · Solution Architecture 06 / 11
Role hierarchy

Everyone sees
precisely what
their role permits.

Cloud-native, multi-tenant — from the individual congregation to the national leadership, structured around a clear role hierarchy.
01
Super Administrator
Full access across the SDA network — all districts, all churches. Sets global policy.
02
District Administrator
Manages churches within their district. District-wide reports and broadcasts.
03
Pastor
Oversees individual church(es) — congregation data, comms, events, attendance.
04
Church Leader
Ministry-specific access — groups, volunteers, or departments within a church.
05
Member
Personal profile, event registration, and direct communication from leadership.
04 · System Usage
How it works

From registration to data-driven decision — every step connected.

01
Member Registers
A new member creates a profile through their church. Data is captured securely into the central registry.
02
Attendance Logged
Pastors mark weekly attendance. The system builds a live engagement record per member over time.
03
Alerts Triggered
If a member misses services consecutively, the system flags them automatically for pastoral follow-up.
04
Leadership Broadcasts
Admins send announcements and event invites — reaching all members instantly across channels.
05
Data Drives Decisions
Dashboards show growth trends, district comparisons, and engagement rates — real leadership intelligence.
04B · System Usage
A day in the life

CCMS adapts to every user.

Member
Views personal profile and membership status
Registers for upcoming church events with one tap
Receives announcements and notices from leadership
Submits prayer requests or updates contact info
Pastor
Opens dashboard to see who has been absent 3+ weeks
Reviews new member registrations and approves profiles
Sends a targeted message to a specific ministry group
Checks weekly attendance summary before service ends
District Admin
Views district-wide membership and attendance map
Broadcasts a district event to all churches at once
Compares engagement metrics across churches in district
Generates a monthly report for union leadership review
Super Admin
Monitors all districts and churches from a single view
Configures global roles, permissions, and access policies
Reviews national growth trends and flags underperforming regions
Manages system settings, security, and user administration
